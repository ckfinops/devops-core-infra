Who This Course is For

The course is designed for professionals who want to understand how FinOps is applied to enhance business value through cloud operations. You should consider this course if you are any of the following:

FinOps Core Personas

Finance, Procurement, and Product professionals who work with FinOps Practitioners at their organization. (Engineers should take the FinOps Certified Engineer course, and Leadership should take the Introduction to FinOps course.)

Allied Personas

Professionals from Intersecting Disciplines such as ITAM, ITFM, Sustainability, and Security who work with FinOps Practitioners

Sales & Marketing

Professionals who work for FinOps tooling vendors (e.g., Product Marketing Manager, Account Executive, Solution Architect)

Product Managers

Professionals who develop FinOps tools for FinOps tooling vendors

Industry Analysts

FinOps Industry Analysts who want a better understanding of the practice
